# Privacy Policy

1.Camera & Scanning

    SpatioCraft uses your device camera and LiDAR sensor solely to capture images for 3d reconstuction.
    No images or scan data are ever transmitted to our servers. All processing happens on your device. 

2.No account required

    SpatioCraft Operates on a pay-per-scan model. you are never asked to create an account, sign in or provide any kind.
    There is no registration, no profile, and no persistent identity tied to your usage.

3.Data Storage

    ALl 3d models, scan images, checkpoints, and exported files are stored exclusively on your device's local storage.
    SpatioCraft does not upload, sync, or backup any of your content to external servers or cloud services. 

4.Exports and Sharing

    When you export a model (USDZ, GLB, gITF, OBJ, STL), the file is written to your device's temporary storage and
    and shared via the standard iOS sharesheet. What you do with the exported file is entirely under your control.

5.Purchases

    Scan credits are processed throgh apple's StoreKit / App Store in-app-purchases system.
    SpatioCraft never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information.
    Transaction records are handled exclusively by apple

6.Analytics and Sharing

    SpatioCraft does not use any third-party Analytics, advertising SDKs, or Tracking frameworks. No usage data, crash reports
    , or behavioral telemetry are collected or shared with any third party.

7.Contact

    if you have any questions about this privacy policy, or SpatioCraft handles your data, please contact us through
    the App Store Support Link on our App page.
